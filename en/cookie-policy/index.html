Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Cookie Policy describes exactly which cookies are used by www.studiodentisticolm.it, operated by Centro Dentistico LM s.a.s., in accordance with GDPR (EU) 2016/679 and applicable Italian regulations.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in the user’s browser during navigation. They allow websites to remember information between visits.

2. Cookies used by this site

This is a static showcase website. It does not use:

  • Own profiling cookies
  • Analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Social plugins (Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
  • Contact forms or login systems

Any session cookies set by the hosting server are strictly technical and do not require consent.

3. Third-party services and their cookies

Google Maps

An interactive map is available on the site. It does not load automatically: it is only activated after the user clicks “Accept and load map”. Only then does the browser connect to Google and Google Maps may set its own cookies (including profiling cookies from Google LLC).

  • Type: third-party (Google LLC)
  • Activation: only on explicit user consent
  • Consent required: yes — via in-page button
  • How to revoke consent: click "Revoke Google Maps consent" in the site footer — the map is immediately deactivated and consent removed. Cookies already set by Google must be deleted manually via your browser settings (see section 4).
  • Google Maps privacy: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Managing cookies

You can manage cookies via your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
