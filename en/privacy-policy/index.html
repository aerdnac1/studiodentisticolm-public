2. What this site collects

This is a static showcase website with no registration, login area, behavioural tracking or social plugins. Data collected is limited to:

  • Technical browsing data: IP address, browser type, OS, pages visited and access times — automatically collected by the hosting server to ensure site operation, deleted within 30 days.
  • Data voluntarily provided via direct contact: if you contact the studio by e-mail or phone (links provided on the site), the data you provide is used solely to respond to your request.

The site does not use contact forms, online booking, analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics) or own profiling cookies. No health data is collected through this website.

3. Legal basis

  • Technical operation of the site — legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Responding to contact requests — consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).
  • Google Maps — explicit user consent before loading (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

4. Data retention

Technical browsing data is retained for a maximum of 30 days. Contact data (e-mail, phone) is retained only for the time necessary to handle the request, or for as long as required by applicable regulations (up to 10 years for healthcare records).

5. Your rights

Under Arts. 15-22 GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to the processing of your personal data, and to data portability. You may also withdraw consent at any time. To exercise your rights, contact us at info@studiodentisticolm.it.

You may also lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority. For Italy: Garante per la protezione dei dati personali. For other EU countries: European Data Protection Board.

This site uses technically necessary cookies and, with your consent, analytical cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.
