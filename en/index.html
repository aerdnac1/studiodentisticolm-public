Our services

Your oral health
step by step

Prevention and oral hygiene

Prevention and Oral Hygiene: professional oral hygiene (scaling, check-ups, fissure sealants, fluoride prophylaxis and oral hygiene education)

Prevention is the foundation of a healthy mouth. Through regular check-ups and professional oral hygiene sessions, we help patients prevent cavities, gingivitis and other oral diseases. We educate adults and children on proper daily hygiene to keep their smile healthy over time.

Restorative Dentistry

Restorative Dentistry: aesthetic fillings and dental restorations

We treat teeth damaged by decay or trauma, preserving as much of the natural tooth structure as possible. We use high-quality aesthetic materials for functional and long-lasting fillings and restorations.

Endodontics

Endodontics: devitalisations, root canal treatments and endodontic retreatments

When decay reaches the inner part of the tooth, we intervene with precise and painless root canal treatments. Our goal is to eliminate the infection, save the natural tooth and restore its functionality.

Prosthetics

Prosthetics: fixed and removable

We create custom fixed and removable prostheses, designed to ensure comfort, aesthetics and functionality. Each solution is tailored to restore a natural smile and proper chewing.

Implantology

Implantology: dental implants, immediate loading implantology and bone regeneration

Dental implants are a safe and long-lasting solution for replacing one or more missing teeth. Thanks to modern techniques and high-quality materials, we restore aesthetics and functionality in a stable and reliable way.

Orthodontics

Orthodontics: invisible aligners

We correct dental misalignments and chewing problems in children, adolescents and adults. We offer solutions such as invisible orthodontic treatments, designed to combine effectiveness and discretion.

Periodontology

Periodontology: treatment of gingivitis, periodontitis, root planing and periodontal surgery

We treat gum diseases such as gingivitis and periodontitis, with targeted therapies to preserve the health of the tooth's supporting tissues and prevent tooth loss.

Oral Surgery

Oral Surgery: extractions in complete safety

We perform oral surgery procedures such as simple and complex extractions, including wisdom teeth, in a safe and controlled environment.

Aesthetic Dentistry

Aesthetic Dentistry: teeth whitening and dental veneers

We improve the aesthetics of the smile through treatments such as teeth whitening and veneers, to achieve whiter, more harmonious and natural-looking teeth.
